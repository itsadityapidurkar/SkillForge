Interview Preparation Guide

Tips and tricks to ace your technical and HR interviews!

Technical Interview Tips

  • Understand the fundamentals of programming languages.
  • Practice solving coding problems on platforms like LeetCode or HackerRank.
  • Review data structures and algorithms thoroughly.
  • Be prepared to explain your past projects and their architecture.

HR Interview Tips

  • Be confident and maintain a positive attitude.
  • Prepare answers to common questions like "Tell me about yourself" and "What are your strengths and weaknesses?".
  • Research the company and its values beforehand.
  • Ask insightful questions to show genuine interest in the role.

General Interview Advice

  • Dress professionally and arrive on time.
  • Bring multiple copies of your resume.
  • Stay calm and take your time to answer questions thoughtfully.
  • Practice mock interviews with friends or mentors.
